Why Satoshium Store Exists

Technology projects often live entirely in code and infrastructure. Satoshium Store exists to give the ecosystem a visible and tangible expression. Through merchandise, symbols, and artifacts, the project becomes something people can hold, wear, share, and represent in the real world.

This domain is the cultural and economic layer of the Satoshium ecosystem.

The Satoshium Domain Architecture

Satoshium Store is one layer within a broader multi-domain ecosystem. Each domain has a distinct role, allowing the project to separate public presence, experiments, infrastructure, knowledge, and commerce.

  • satoshium.ai — public-facing platform
  • satoshium-progress — development progress and public build surface
  • satoshium.info — documentation and knowledge layer
  • satoshium.xyz — experimental systems and frontier concepts
  • satoshium.net — infrastructure and network layer
  • satoshium.store — merchandise and economic layer
Together, these domains form a layered architecture rather than a single flat website.

Current Workspace Functions

  • Architecture — ecosystem role, relationships, and planning notes

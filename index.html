Current Status

Satoshium Store is currently in its early conceptual stage. The structure of the domain has been established, but the merchandise layer will grow gradually alongside the development of the broader Satoshium ecosystem.

Future releases may include apparel, collectibles, artwork, and symbolic items representing milestones in the evolution of Satoshium.

Satoshium is being built slowly, in public, and with architectural discipline.
